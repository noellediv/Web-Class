Why you should get a Boxer?

Boxer Characteristics!

  • Boxers are loyal to their families.
  • Boxers learn their training quickly.
  • Boxers get along with other dogs.
  • Boxers are lovers not fighters

What you need to know before adopting a Boxer today!

  • Boxers can get very excited and enthusiastic but always mean well.
  • Boxers require lots of activity.
  • They can have a variety of health issues: allergies, skin problems, and heart conditions are some popular ones.
  • Boxers do shed hair.
  • Boxers can be very messy eaters.

Boxer are an energetic, fun loving dog breed and with the right care they can prosper in you household. They are great with kids and many other animals. They are family oriented dogs. Boxers are great... so adopt yours today!
